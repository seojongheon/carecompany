# Compound Knowledge Record Template

Use this template to preserve durable project knowledge after an incident, decision, migration, or significant verification effort.

## YYYY-MM-DD — Title

1. **Scope**
   - What system, feature, or operation is affected?
2. **Symptom and impact**
   - What happened and who or what was affected?
3. **Evidence**
   - What logs, tests, measurements, or observations support the finding?
4. **Decision and cause**
   - What was determined and why?
5. **Change or operational action**
   - What was changed or done?
6. **Verification and follow-up**
   - What evidence confirms the result, and what remains?

## Safety

Do not record credentials, tokens, private keys, personal data, raw IP addresses, or secret-bearing logs.
